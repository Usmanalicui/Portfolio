Key Events, Policies, and Disruptions in Pakistan (2016‑2018) Impacting Online Sales
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sep 28‑30, 2016 – Pakistan’s Great Online Shopping Festival 2016 (3 days)
- Boosted awareness of online shopping, increased traffic and sales.

Nov 25, 2016 – Daraz Black Friday 2016 (“Pakistan’s first one billion rupee sale”) (1 day, extended deals may last 2-3 days)
- Major milestone sale, high discounts, boosted adoption.

Dec 26, 2016 – PTCL Internet / Cellular Outage (~5 hours)
- Large outage across Sindh, Balochistan, Punjab. Disrupted sales, payments, and online activity.

July 1, 2017 – New 0.5% Minimum Tax for Online Marketplaces (Permanent, regulatory)
- Extra compliance burden, affects profitability.

Late 2017 – Finance Act 2017 Definitions & Tax Adjustments (Permanent, regulatory)
- Defined “online marketplace,” adjusted tax rates.

June 2018 – Third Devaluation of Pakistani Rupee in FY18 (Ongoing economic impact, not a fixed duration)
- Raised cost of imports, increased consumer prices.

2016‑2018 (Various) – Internet / Connectivity Disruptions (Hours to full days depending on outage)
- Reduced consumer trust, directly disrupted ordering and payment systems.

2016‑2018 (General Economic Pressure) (Ongoing)
- Inflation, rising import costs, weaker purchasing power affected demand.


Additional E-Commerce and Sale Events in Pakistan (2016‑2018)
-------------------------------------------------------------

Great Online Shopping Festival (GOSF.Live) – 28-30 September 2016 (3 days)
- A 3-day online shopping festival by Jang Media + Google with 50+ partners.

Daraz Black Friday 2016 – 25 November 2016 (1 day, some deals extended ~3 days)
- Major sale event with steep discounts, heavy marketing.

MyGerrys “Biggest Online Sale of the Year” – 22-27 November 2016 (6 days)
- Discounts up to 85%, focus on “Cash on Delivery.”

Pakistan Shopping Day – 19 May 2017 (1 day)
- Initiated by Yayvo, held before Ramzan. Heavy discounts and promos.

Eid‑ul‑Fitr Festive / Eid Collection Sales – June 2017 (2-3 weeks leading up to Eid)
- Clothing brands launched festive collections, big discounts.

Eid‑ul‑Azha 2017 Sale – August / Early September 2017 (1-2 weeks before Eid)
- Shoppingbag.pk and others ran sales with discounts up to 50%.
