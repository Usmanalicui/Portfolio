Additional E-Commerce and Sale Events in Pakistan (2016‑2018)

Great Online Shopping Festival (GOSF.Live) – 28-30 September 2016
- A 3-day online shopping festival by Jang Media + Google with 50+ e‑commerce partners. Big discounts across many categories.

Daraz Black Friday 2016 – 25 November 2016
- Major sale event with steep discounts, high traffic, partnerships (e.g. with Zong), and strong marketing campaigns.

MyGerrys “Biggest Online Sale of the Year” – 22-27 November 2016
- Discounts up to 85%, focused especially on “Cash on Delivery” purchases.

Pakistan Shopping Day – 19 May 2017
- Launched by Yayvo + partners. A major online shopping day before Ramzan and Eid. Featured big discounts, bank promotions, and telecom offers.

Eid‑ul‑Fitr Festive / Eid Collection Sales – June 2017 (just before Eid‑ul‑Fitr)
- Fashion and clothing brands (PakStyle.pk, Gul Ahmed, etc.) launched festive collections, offering discounts on clothing, shoes, and accessories.

Eid‑ul‑Azha 2017 Sale – August / Early September 2017
- Shoppingbag.pk and other stores launched Eid‑ul‑Azha sales with discounts up to 50% on imported items, household, and kitchen products.
